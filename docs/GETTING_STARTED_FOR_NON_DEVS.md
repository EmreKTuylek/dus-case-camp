# Getting Started for DUS Case Camp (Admin Guide)

This guide explains how to manage the DUS Case Camp platform without writing code.

## 1. Accessing the Admin Panel
1.  Log in to the app with your teacher account.
2.  Go to the **Profile** tab.
3.  Click the **Admin Panel** button (red icon).

## 2. Creating a New Week
1.  In the Admin Panel, go to the **Weeks** tab (calendar icon).
2.  Click the **+** button.
3.  Enter a title (e.g., "Week 1: Endodontics").
4.  Select the Start and End dates.
5.  Click **Create**.
6.  **Important**: The week is created as "Inactive". Do not activate it until you have added cases.

## 3. Adding Cases
1.  In the **Weeks** list, click the "Edit Note" icon next to the week you just created.
2.  You are now in the **Cases** list for that week.
3.  Click the **+** button.
4.  Fill in the details:
    *   **Title**: e.g., "Root Canal Complication"
    *   **Description**: Detailed clinical scenario.
    *   **Speciality**: e.g., "Endo", "Perio".
    *   **Difficulty**: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
5.  Click **Create**.

## 4. Launching the Week
1.  Go back to the **Weeks** tab.
2.  Find your new week.
3.  Click **Activate**.
4.  **Result**:
    *   This week becomes visible to all students on the Home Screen.
    *   A push notification is sent to all students: "New Week Started!".

## 5. Reviewing & Scoring Submissions
1.  In the Admin Panel, go to the **Reviews** tab (rate icon).
2.  You will see a list of "Pending" submissions.
3.  Click on a submission to review it.
4.  **Watch the video**.
5.  **Enter a Score** (0-100).
6.  (Optional) Enter feedback.
7.  Click **Save Score**.
8.  **Result**:
    *   The student gets points (Score * 0.5).
    *   The student receives a notification.
    *   Leaderboards are updated automatically.

## 6. Leaderboards
*   **Weekly Leaderboard**: Shows top students for the current active week.
*   **All Time Leaderboard**: Shows top students across all weeks.
*   These update automatically whenever you save a score.
